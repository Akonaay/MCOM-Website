About Us

A Tanzanian based company determined in building a reliable communication network by providing cutting edge technology in Broadband Wireless and Fiber Optics for guaranteed access of data and voice.

M COM Ltd introduces itself as a partner in building a reliable network by providing competitive technology and price on the required Wireless or Fiber infrastructure.

Learn More

M COM started as a provider of one of Inmarsat services, BGAN. Delivered via the most advanced commercial communication satellite ever launched, BGAN provides affordable, mobile broadband services at speed up to half a megabit in a highly compact device. It is designed for simplicity and ease to use. No specialist technical expertise is required to set up and use it. Industries where BGAN could be used include,

  • Construction
  • Mining and gas exploration
  • Military
  • Tourism
  • Media
